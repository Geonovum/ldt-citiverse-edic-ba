# Capabilities and Enablement

Capabilities represent the "link" between strategic goals and the operational play of the organization. Capabilities are the 'things' the organisation (EDIC) is, or must be, able to do to reach its strategic goals.

## 4.1 Core Business Capabilities
*   **Ecosystem Orchestration:** The ability to coordinate multiple Member States and technology providers.
*   **Data Governance & Stewardship:** Managing trusted data sharing through Data Spaces (e.g., DS4SSCC, SIMPL) .
*   **Interoperability Management:** Profiling and promoting technical standards to reduce fragmentation.

## 4.2 Organizational Competencies
*   **Strategic Advisory:** Helping cities navigate EU funding and digital transformation roadmaps.
*   **Validation & Benchmarking:** Establishing frameworks to test the quality and reliability of LDT simulations.

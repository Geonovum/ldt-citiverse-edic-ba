# Application and infrastructure layer

## Sandboxes
The EDIC will make use of technical infrastructure, called Sandboxes, for two main purposes.

### Testing and validation platform
LDT-components delivered by EU-projects, strategic partners, national hubs, etc. will be validated against the strategic goals of the EDIC and the defined adoption criteria. The technical components will be tested on a Sandbox environment, which at least will include the test of the factual interoperability with the other components in the EDIC products and services catalog. 
By doing this, the EDIC also plays a role as an integration layer across EU digitalisation programs.
### Demonstrations
A second Sandbox environment will be available for members to demonstrate the value of (network) digital twins and of individual components for use in specific usecases. 

<aside class="note">
    Currently the EDIC is not considering to be a hosting party. Cities and regions have to implement their digital twins in their own environments, which may, of course, be on a third party's infrastructure.
</aside>

Decision Support ()
> Carefully weighing effects and interests and subsequently recording a choice based on the insights provided by the digital twin. This involves weighing different interests and impacts (e.g., livability vs. cost) and recording a choice. By linking decisions to the underlying data, it remains clear why a choice was made, allowing for later accountability or revision.
